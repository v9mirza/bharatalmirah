Frequently Asked Questions

Do you support bulk and institutional orders?

Yes. We manufacture directly, allowing us to handle everything from individual bespoke orders to massive bulk requirements for schools, hostels, offices, and commercial projects across India.

Can I request a custom size or layout?

Absolutely. Because we control the production process, custom options—including unique dimensioning and specialized internal layouts—are available depending on quantity and design feasibility.

How should I choose the right model?

Simply share your user count, primary storage type, and available room space with us via the inquiry form. Our concierge team will suggest the most efficient, durable model combinations to maximize your space.
